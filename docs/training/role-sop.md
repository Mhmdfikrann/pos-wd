# Role SOP & Training

Phase 12 training material for launch. Each trainee should complete the flow on
the production-like environment before live traffic.

## Kasir

Daily flow:

1. Login with assigned account/PIN.
2. Open shift with counted opening cash.
3. Create dine-in/takeaway order.
4. Add/remove cart items and verify total.
5. Complete cash payment and verify change.
6. Complete non-cash payment and verify receipt.
7. Confirm kitchen ticket appears after payment.
8. Request refund/void/discount with reason when needed.
9. Close shift, count actual cash, submit note for discrepancy.

Do not:

- Share PIN.
- Reuse another cashier shift.
- Retry payment by refreshing the page; use the visible result/receipt first.

## Dapur

Daily flow:

1. Login to kitchen screen.
2. Filter by station if needed.
3. Move tickets through accepted/preparing/ready/completed.
4. Confirm cancelled/voided orders no longer require preparation.
5. Escalate stale tickets to Manager.

Do not:

- Change status for tickets outside the station unless assigned.
- Prepare from screenshots without verifying ticket status.

## Manager

Daily flow:

1. Review open shifts and operational KPIs.
2. Review approval inbox.
3. Approve/reject refund, void, and discount requests with reason.
4. Check transaction history after approval.
5. Monitor cash close differences.
6. Confirm backup status during launch week.

Do not:

- Approve requests without checking transaction number and outlet.
- Use Owner credentials for outlet operations.

## Inventory

Daily flow:

1. Review low/out stock.
2. Record stock in/out/adjustment with note.
3. Verify stock ledger after adjustment.
4. Confirm recipe deductions after checkout.
5. Escalate negative-stock or missing-recipe issues.

Do not:

- Adjust stock without physical count or note.
- Delete/replace audit evidence outside the system.

## Owner

Daily flow:

1. Review Dashboard Penjualan.
2. Check date/outlet-scoped reports.
3. Compare daily sales, payment mix, cashier report, inventory value, and refund/void/discount events.
4. Review launch incident log.
5. Approve rollback decisions if needed.
